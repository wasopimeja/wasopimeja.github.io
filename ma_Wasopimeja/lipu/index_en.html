Welcome to Wasopimea

Vasopimea is an enclave state in Eastern Europe, completely surrounded by the territory of Ukraine. The official language of Vasopimea is Tokipona. The capital of the country is the city of Pokatelo. The state was founded on 06.01.2023. Vapa is currently the Country Manager.
